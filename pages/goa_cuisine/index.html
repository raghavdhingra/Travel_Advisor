Welcome

If you are in North Goa and near to Panjim ktc bus stand then this is one of the best restaurant's near by. They are mainly famous for their Fish thali. This is comparatively small sized restaurant and always almost crowded. Fish thali includes 7-8 item's including crabs, fish fry fish gravy,rice, chapati,papad n many more. We even ordered Crab soup and Chicken tikka. For both quantity and quantity was satisfactory. All in all, nice restaurant to have fish thali. Highly recommended.
